            <h2 class="subtitle"><big>Introduction</big></h2>

            <div class="content">
                <p><strong>The Boys' Brigade (BB)</strong> or <strong>Briged Putera</strong> is one of the uniform group that recognized by the Ministry of Youth and Sports and also the Ministry of Education as an approved Extra Curricular Activity (ECA).</p>
                <p><strong>But what makes Boys’ Brigade so differ</strong> from other uniform group is that we not only teach the members about discipline and skills. Boys’ Brigade is formed by two pillars, which is discipline and also Christianity. Therefore, we shape the character of the members by sharing gospel to our members at the same time make them to know discipline and obedience through our program such as parade meeting, which happens on every Saturday at the Church of Good Shepherds.</p>
                <p><strong>Praise to the Lord</strong> with the support of the church, our 5th Sandakan Company can keep on running the Boys’ Brigade Ministry until today. Come and join us, experience life changed with the fellowship and bonding with 5th Sandakan Company!</p>
                <p><strong>The Object of BB (original)</strong> “The advancement of Christ’s kingdom among Boys and the promotion of habits of Obedience, Reverence, Discipline, Self-respect and all that tends towards a true Christian manliness.”</p>
                <p><strong>In Malaysia,</strong> the Object has been adapted to be gender neutral as we have girls in BB.  “The advancement of Christ’s kingdom among Members and the promotion of habits of Obedience, Reverence, Discipline, Self-respect and all that tends towards a true Christian character.”</p>
            </div>
